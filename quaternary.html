Lady Macbeth

When you durst do it, then you were a man;
And, to be more than what you were, you would be so much more the man.

-- Lady Macbeth(1.7.49)

Lady Macbeth's definition, however, is much more specific and violent than Macbeth's simple "man dares". She first talks about being a man as early as Act 1 Scene 7.
